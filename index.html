'The Lockdown Project'

  • Full body panel restoration and repaint

Approved Installation Partner

We offer approved installation services for:

LOF Clutches

We fit the Road, Power, or Extreme spec clutches from LOF Clutches- these are long life and high performance alternatives to the factory-fitted clutches.

Pioneer 4x4

We use stainless steel braided brake lines, and one-piece clutch lines (with a huge choice of colours!) from Pioneer 4x4. They even do custom hoses for any type of build!

Ratel-X

We love Ratel-X's kit, using their diff guards, steering guards, and recovery points as standard - but can fit anything from their range of products.
