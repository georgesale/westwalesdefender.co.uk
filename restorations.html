Pricing

"How much?" is the most important question, and one we hear often! The answer is - it depends!

We have fixed prices and set labour rates for as many jobs as we can, and we'll tell you what might be variable - up front. Most other restorers won't tell you, or will split out costs - but we believe with such an important project, you should be in control.

We only charge for labour, and don't make profit on parts - we'll even tell you which parts are needed, so you can source and send direct to us. We'll let you know when a range of options are available - and what might be a good idea (or not!).

We also provide an assessment service - we'll spend some time looking at your Land Rover and let you know what we recommend doing - with various options. This is free when you take a restoration, and around £50 as a standalone service.

Rolling Chassis

During the restoration process, we will get your Land Rover down to a 'rolling chassis'. Without doubt - this is the best time to upgrade loads of bits - and you'll save money doing it at this time, whilst it's apart and things are easy to change. We provide a fixed price list for adding upgrades here - polybush, new suspension, new brake and clutch lines, etc. Full details below.

Vision

We can bring your Defender back to 'factory fresh' or transform it - we even do conversions to/from pickup, station wagon, soft-top, and panel van - adding or removing windows or cab options. As they say - it's your party- so let us know what you're looking for, and we can bring it to life. At this stage, we can talk through options with you, and plan the restoration.

Axles

Depending on the condition of your axles, we can repair or full restore - with a full restoration (parts included) costing about £2800. With a full restoration, we do the following:

  • sand blast & powder coat casings
  • new oil seals and gaskets
  • new (heavy duty) solid half shafts
  • swivels if needed inc. oil seals
  • stainless YRM brackets

We also provide options to upgrade from drum to disk brakes

Engine, Clutch, Gear Box, & Transfer Box

We start with a steam clean, and go from there - usually repainting the engine in the factory colour (gold??) (or, whatever you like). Depending on the condition of your engine (we find most are fairly good), we do a full service by a qualified ex-Land Rover technician (~£185) or rebuild with skim/rebore/gaskets for around £1800. For a full historic rebuild of a Series engine, contact us for details (this can be up to £5k).

At this stage, we do the clutch, gear box, and transfer box. We offer approved partner upgrades to LOF Clutches (3 types depending on intended usage). At this point, we can configure other options, such as:

  • new pipes, silicon hoses
  • engine and axle breather kits for wading (fixed price)
  • brake and clutch hoses from (LOF??) with colour options
  • airbox upgrades for Puma Defenders
  • gearbox and transfer box repairs/upgrades from IRB Developments
